Signs You Need AC Repair

Is your AC acting up? Here are some common signs that it's time to call for repairs:

  • Weak or no airflow from vents
  • Strange noises like clanking, banging, or scraping
  • Thermostat not working properly
  • Hot spots around your home
  • Water leaking around your AC unit
  • High humidity inside your home

If you notice any of these issues, don't wait! The sooner you get repairs, the less likely you'll face a complete breakdown or expensive replacement.

Common AC Problems in Henderson

Henderson's hot climate can be tough on air conditioners. Here are some frequent issues local HVAC technicians encounter:

  1. Dirty air filters: These can restrict airflow and make your AC work harder.
  2. Refrigerant leaks: Low refrigerant levels can cause your AC to blow warm air.
  3. Frozen evaporator coils: This can happen due to dirty filters or low refrigerant.
  4. Faulty compressors: The "heart" of your AC system can wear out over time.
  5. Clogged condensate drains: This can lead to water leaks and potential damage.

DIY AC Maintenance Tips

While major repairs should be left to professionals, there are some things you can do to keep your AC running smoothly:

  1. Change air filters regularly: Every 1-3 months, depending on usage.
  2. Keep outdoor unit clear: Remove debris and trim plants around the unit.
  3. Clean air vents: Vacuum or wipe down vents to improve airflow.
  4. Check thermostat batteries: Replace if needed for accurate temperature control.
  5. Schedule annual maintenance: Professional tune-ups can prevent costly breakdowns.

When to Replace vs. Repair Your AC

Sometimes, repairing an old AC unit isn't the most cost-effective option. Consider replacement if:

  • Your AC is over 10-15 years old
  • Repairs would cost more than half the price of a new unit
  • Your energy bills keep rising despite repairs
  • You're constantly calling for repairs

Many Henderson AC companies offer free consultations to help you decide between repair and replacement.
