FAQs: Your SEER Questions Answered!¬†

Q: What's a good SEER rating?

A: While 14-16 is good, aim for 16+ for the best energy savings!

Q: How do I find my AC's SEER rating?

A: Check the yellow EnergyGuide label on your outdoor unit or look up your model number online.

Q: Is it worth upgrading from 14 SEER to 16 SEER?

A: Yes! You could save up to 13% on cooling costs. That adds up fast!

Q: What's the difference between SEER and EER?

A: SEER measures seasonal efficiency, while EER looks at peak day performance. SEER gives a better overall picture for most homeowners.
