About Best In The West

Who We Are: North Las Vegas' Premier Air Conditioning & HVAC Contractor

At Best In The West Air Conditioning, we deliver expert air conditioning and HVAC solutions to homes and businesses throughout North Las Vegas since 2017. As a full-service air conditioning and HVAC contractor, we specialize in keeping North Las Vegas comfortable through even the most extreme desert temperatures.

Why Choose Us?

✅ 15+ Years of Experience – Our founder, Ivan Luis, began his HVAC career at the age of 15. With over a decade and a half of expertise, we've developed the skills to handle any air conditioning challenge.

✅ Transparent Pricing – We provide clear, upfront pricing on all services. No hidden fees, just honest air conditioning repair and installation at competitive rates.

✅ Comprehensive Service Range – From residential AC repairs to complete commercial HVAC installations, our certified technicians deliver quality results for every project.

A Message from Ivan Luis, Founder of Best In The West Air Conditioning:

"I started working in HVAC when I was just 15. I’ve seen it all—from fixing residential units to heavy commercial systems. In 2017, I decided to start my own company to offer honest, affordable, and expert HVAC services right here in North Las Vegas. I’m proud to say that we’re faster, more experienced, and more reliable than any other HVAC contractor in town. When your system breaks down, call us. You won’t be disappointed."
