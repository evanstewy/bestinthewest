Signs You Need Refrigerant Recharge

Is your AC system showing these warning signs? Don't wait until complete system failure:

  • AC blowing warm or lukewarm air despite the thermostat being set to cool
  • Longer cooling cycles with your system running continuously
  • Higher than normal energy bills without increased usage
  • Hissing or bubbling sounds coming from your AC unit or refrigerant lines
  • Ice formation on refrigerant lines or the evaporator coil
  • Poor airflow from your vents when the AC is running
  • Home feels humid even with the air conditioner running

If you notice any of these symptoms, call us immediately at (702) 682-8439 for professional refrigerant service.

Our Refrigerant Recharge Process

At Best In The West Air Conditioning, we follow a comprehensive process to ensure your AC system receives the proper refrigerant service:

  1. Professional System Diagnosis - Our EPA-certified technicians perform a thorough inspection to verify refrigerant levels and identify potential leaks.
  2. Leak Detection & Repair - If refrigerant is low, we locate and repair any leaks before recharging. This prevents future issues and protects the environment.
  3. Proper Refrigerant Recharge - We use precise equipment to add the manufacturer-specified type and amount of refrigerant for your system model.
  4. System Performance Testing - After recharging, we test your system's cooling performance, ensuring proper operation and efficiency.
  5. Final Inspection - We check all components to confirm everything is working correctly and that your system maintains proper refrigerant pressure.
