DIY Maintenance Tips to Prevent AC Repairs

Simple steps to keep your AC running smoothly:

  • Keep the outdoor unit clear of debris.
  • Clean air vents regularly.
  • Check thermostat batteries to ensure proper function.
  • Schedule annual maintenance with a professional.
