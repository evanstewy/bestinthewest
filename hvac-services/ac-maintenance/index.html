Signs Your AC System Needs Maintenance

Watch for these warning signs that indicate your system needs professional attention:

  • Strange noises like banging, hissing, or grinding 🤔
  • Inconsistent temperatures or hot/cold spots throughout your home 🌡️
  • Unexplained increases in your energy bills 📈
  • Poor indoor air quality or increased dust 🍃
  • System constantly cycling on and off ♻️
  • Reduced airflow from vents 🌬️

Maintenance vs. Repair: The Smart Investment

Consider these facts:

  • Regular maintenance costs a fraction of emergency repairs ⚡
  • Well-maintained systems use up to 30% less energy 💰
  • Preventative maintenance extends system lifespan by 3-5 years 📅
  • Most major AC breakdowns show warning signs that maintenance catches 🔍
