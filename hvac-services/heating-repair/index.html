Quick Emergency Tips for Heating Issues

Try these simple fixes before calling for service:

  • Check your thermostat batteries and settings
  • Inspect your circuit breaker for tripped switches
  • Replace your air filter (a common cause of heating problems)
  • Ensure all vents are open and unobstructed
  • Listen for unusual noises that can indicate specific problems
