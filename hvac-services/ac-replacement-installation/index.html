Maximizing Your New AC System Efficiency

Simple steps to get the most from your new installation:

  • Install a programmable thermostat for automatic temperature control.
  • Ensure proper home insulation to maintain cool air.
  • Use ceiling fans to help distribute cool air efficiently.
  • Close blinds during peak heat hours to reduce solar gain.
  • Schedule regular maintenance to keep your system running optimally.

Repair or Replace? When to Upgrade Your AC

Consider a replacement if:

  • Your AC is over 10-15 years old ⏳
  • Repairs cost more than half the price of a new unit 💸
  • Energy bills keep rising despite repairs 📈
  • You're constantly calling for repairs 📞
